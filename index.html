About Us

Ideal for spending your holidays surrounded by greenery and tranquility 🌳 🏠 Completely independent, fully renovated and carefully furnished, a small cottage. Sale of organic honey, fruits, and vegetables. Additionally, for those who wish, there are massage sessions (draining, relaxing, cupping, Tibetan bells), Reiki.. 🌿 Guests have access to an outdoor space with a terrace, barbecue, and a walk in the woods, half an hour from Cuneo and 20 minutes from Saluzzo for those who want to visit these splendid towns and stroll through the market. 🏡 30 minutes from Lake Pontechianale, for those who enjoy hiking, small trails leading to 4 or 7 chapels. The farmhouse "Mélodie" is located in Venasca, province of Cuneo, in the Varaita Valley, in a characteristic mountain village nestled in the woods. 🌲The ideal place to recharge in nature and go hiking in the mountains, where you'll hear only the sound of streams and the song of birds 🏔️ A 10-minute drive from the village, convenient for all services. ✨ The best choice for a relaxing and nature-filled vacation 🥰 If you're interested, please contact me via email.
